    • High-scoring offenses and tight rivalries
    • Regular national TV exposure
  3. The Independence and Big 12 Transition
    • Challenging national schedules
    • Increased visibility against brand-name programs

Game Day in Provo

Cougar fans pack LaVell Edwards Stadium with noise, blue pom-poms, and coordinated chants. The ROC student section sets the tone with energy from kickoff to the final whistle.

From the pregame Cougar Walk to the roar after a long touchdown pass, BYU football games combine tradition, preparation, and passion into one experience.

Key BYU Football Rivalries

Rivalry games are where the stories, emotions, and long-term memories are made. These matchups often define a season.

BYU vs Utah

The “Holy War” is one of the most intense rivalries in college football, with stakes that go far beyond the final score.

BYU vs Utah State

A regional rivalry that has produced close games and memorable finishes, especially in Logan and Provo night games.

BYU vs Boise State

Recent years have seen multiple dramatic games on the blue turf and in Provo, adding a modern edge to BYU’s rivalry history.
